Product Docs

Bag Builders Exchange provides live chart views for SPY and QQQ plus focused scanners designed to help traders read the market faster. This page gives a quick overview of what the product does, how to navigate it, and what to expect.

What Bag Builders Exchange is

Bag Builders Exchange is a market-viewing and scanner platform built for speed, clarity, and readable market context. The product is designed to surface what matters quickly without forcing users to jump across too many tabs or overloaded interfaces.

The platform currently centers around two live chart views and three focused scanners:

  • SPY live chart view
  • QQQ live chart view
  • Impulse Scanner
  • Leap Scanner
  • Swing Scanner

What each tool is for

SPY and QQQ are live chart views intended to help users quickly understand market structure, context, and broad directional behavior. These pages are best used to anchor the session.

Impulse Scanner is focused on names that are actively moving and attracting attention. It is designed for fast intraday discovery.

Leap Scanner is intended for names showing stronger expansion potential or broader move development.

Swing Scanner is designed for setups with a wider time horizon and cleaner multi-session potential.

Data and availability

Product availability and freshness may vary by market session, route, or data source. Certain modules are most useful during regular U.S. market hours, while others may continue to display last-session or after-hours snapshots when the market is closed.

Market hours referenced on the platform are Monday through Friday, 9:30 AM to 4:00 PM ET.

Important note

Bag Builders Exchange is for educational and informational purposes only. It does not place trades, execute brokerage activity, or provide personalized investment advice.
