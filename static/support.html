Support

How can we help?

Get help with membership, billing, account access, product questions, display issues, bug reports, and general feedback.

Contact support

Email the Bag Builders Exchange team and include enough detail for us to understand what happened.

Support email support@bagbuildersexchange.com

Use the email address connected to your account whenever possible.

What to include

  • Your name or account email
  • The page, route, or tool you were using
  • A short description of what happened
  • A screenshot or screen recording, when applicable
  • The approximate time the issue occurred
Email Support Return to Account

Common topics

These are the issues we can help investigate.

  • Membership and billing Plan changes, cancellations, payment methods, and invoices.
  • Account access Login problems, restricted tools, or account status questions.
  • Display and data issues Chart rendering, stale prices, layout issues, or broken UI.
  • Product questions Questions about SPY, QQQ, Impulse, Leap, or Board Scale tools.
  • Feedback Feature requests and suggestions for product improvements.

Response timing

Response times may vary based on message volume, market hours, and the type of issue reported.

Support is provided on a best-effort basis while the platform continues to grow.
